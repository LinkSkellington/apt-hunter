📖 How to read this dashboard Show ▾
⭐ Favorites
Listings you've starred. Your personal shortlist of apartments worth revisiting.
👀 For Review
Listings you've flagged for your wife to review. Click 👀 on any card to send it here.
🗓 New Today
Everything added in the most recent scrape run. Check this first each morning.
🆕 Just Listed
Fresh listings (≤7 days old) with a score ≥55. New to market and worth a look — act before they go.
🔥 Top Picks
Scores ≥80, all must-have criteria pass, commute ≤45 min. These check every box — prioritize touring these.
💪 So Close
Scores 45–67. Good options that are close but may have an unknown field, slightly longer commute, or fewer amenities.
📋 All Active
Every listing in the database that hasn't been rejected. Use neighborhood filters to narrow down.
🔢 Score
0–100 based on: commute (20pts), neighborhood (15pts), hard criteria (30pts), sqft (10pts), light (10pts), amenities (10pts), building (5pts).
Pipeline runs automatically at 7am, 12pm, 5pm, and 10pm ET via GitHub Actions — no manual work needed. The dashboard auto-refreshes every 5 minutes.
